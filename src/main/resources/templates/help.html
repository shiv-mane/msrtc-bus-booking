How to book a bus ticket?

To book a bus ticket, follow these steps:

  1. Go to our website and click on the "Book Now" button.
  2. Select your desired source and destination locations.
  3. Choose the date and time of travel.
  4. Select the number of passengers.
  5. Click on the "Search Buses" button.
  6. Choose a bus from the available options.
  7. Provide passenger details and make the payment.
  8. Your bus ticket will be confirmed, and you'll receive a confirmation email.
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Net banking
  • UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
  • Mobile wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay, etc.)
